Support Services to Agriculture

At Kodai Agriorg, we are committed to promoting sustainable agriculture and empowering local communities.
In addition to our community farming services, we offer a range of support services to agriculture and cultivation of plants,
designed to help farmers grow healthy, sustainable crops.
These services include

CULTIVATION OF PLANTS

We offer expert guidance and support to farmers, covering a wide range of topics such as soil health, plant nutrition, pest management, and irrigation.

TREE PLANTATION

When you purchase a farm plot at Kodai Agriorg, we will plant trees per unit of parcel, ensuring that you get the best possible yield.

MAINTENANCE OF FARMLAND

Our team of experts will assist you with planting and maintaining your avocado farmland, ensuring that your trees are healthy and productive.

SUPPLY OF FARM LABOUR

We provide skilled and experienced farm labour to assist with planting, harvesting, and other day-to-day operations.

EARTHWORK OF PLANTATION

We offer earthwork services, including land leveling, clearing, and manuring, to ensure that your farmland is ready for planting.

USE OF NATURAL FERTILIZERS

We use only natural fertilizers and organic farming methods, ensuring that your crops are healthy and sustainable.

Community-Based Hill Side and
Step Farming

With years of experience and expertise in this field, Kodai Agriorg has successfully implemented step farming techniques to cultivate a variety of crops on the slopes of hills in the Kodaikanal region.For hill side and step farming, CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) can be particularly beneficial. These farming practices require a significant amount of labor and resources to create the level platforms and terraces necessary to grow crops on slopes. By participating in CSA, consumers can directly support the farmers who undertake this difficult work and help to sustain these traditional farming practices.
